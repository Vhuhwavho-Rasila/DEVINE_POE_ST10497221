Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we gladly accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies. Please inform us when making your reservation, and our chefs will prepare suitable alternatives.

What is the dress code?

We recommend smart casual attire. While we don't require jackets, we ask guests to avoid beachwear, athletic wear, and flip-flops.

Is there parking available?

Valet parking is available at the restaurant entrance. Alternatively, there is paid parking at the Victoria & Alfred Waterfront parking garage.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, we offer gift certificates that can be purchased in person or over the phone. They make perfect gifts for special occasions.
